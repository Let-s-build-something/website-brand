Most commonly asked questions

Can I choose who I share with?

Yes. You can group people by closeness and decide what each circle (group) sees. This replaces the outdated "friend list" system.

What exactly is being shared?

Just the latest flower state. Calculated as a relative change in your mood compared to the days before. We intend to advance this further in the future: splitting short-term (a flower) and long-term (a tree) changes.

How does automatic analysis work?

It combines music, weather, screen time, and health (smart watch/ring). You associate the different playlists, artists, and apps you use with feelings. Then, in the background, it is evaluated and estimates your mood based on the amount of listening time/use time.

With health, we use custom anonymized machine learning models. Always more to come, especially when it comes to personalization.

The raw data and the context we use is never stored in our databases. Instead, it stays in the app.

What if the analysis is wrong?

Any hour of the day can be altered in your calendar or through a diary. We work on improving our system and bringing personalization, which will significantly improve things in the future.

Is it secure?

Augmy is built on end-to-end encryption. So sharing your mood is the same as sending a message through WhatsApp or Signal.

How do you handle my data?

We don't, and never will, sell any of your data, nor will we provide it to any third party. Whenever possible, your data stays on the device, or it is encrypted en route and minimally stored.

What about therapists?

The most impactful actors in improving mental well-being are you, people around you, and professionals. We intend to bring all three together and focus on motivating all parties involved. For example, these include family plans, monthly recaps, gamification, and professional reviews with personalized recommendations.
